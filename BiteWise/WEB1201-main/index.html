About Us

BiteWise is a mood-based food recommendation platform that helps you discover what to eat based on how you're feeling. Whether you're happy, tired, sad, or just bored, we match your mood to meals that hit the spot.

Our mission is to make food discovery more personal, playful, and emotionally intuitive. We believe food is more than just fuel it's comfort, celebration, and connection. At BiteWise, we want every bite to match your vibe, making each meal a small moment of joy.
